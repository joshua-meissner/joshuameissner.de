About

I'm a freelance writer, engineer, and adventure cyclist based in Berlin.
My long-form profiles, routes, and stories from the heart get published on BIKEPACKING.com.
With my engineering background, I deliver early-stage feedback on prototypes to product teams at various outdoor equipment manufacturers.
I've worked in science communication & PR at Fraunhofer and have experience in technical translations in industry and academia.
