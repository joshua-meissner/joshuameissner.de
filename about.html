About

Joshua Meissner is a freelance writer, photographer, and engineer based in Berlin. His profiles on manufacturing companies in cycling, in-depth gear reviews, routes, and stories from the heart get published on BIKEPACKING.com. He's worked in science communication & PR at Fraunhofer and has experience in technical translations and copywriting. Currently, he's on tour, exploring Europe by bike.
