About

Joshua Meissner is a freelance writer, passionate photographer, engineer, and adventure cyclist based in Berlin.
His profiles on manufacturing companies in cycling, in-depth gear reviews, routes, and stories from the heart get published on BIKEPACKING.com.
With his engineering background, he delivers valued real-world product feedback as a prototype tester for various large outdoor gear companies.
He's worked in science communication & PR at Fraunhofer and has experience in technical translations and copywriting.
